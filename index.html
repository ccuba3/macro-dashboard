## FED WATCH


The Fed at 3.64% is materially behind the curve with CPI at these levels and a war-driven supply shock that monetary policy cannot directly address — a classic cost-push dilemma. The positive 10Y-2Y spread and rising long-end yields suggest the market expects the Fed will be forced to hike into economic weakness, but the 4.3% unemployment rate is likely giving the FOMC cover to delay, risking an even more painful tightening cycle later.
